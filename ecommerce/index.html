Am I eligible to apply?
Companies must make a minimum of $100,000 in monthly sales and have been in operation for at least 1 year to be eligible for Brex’s ecommerce card. Sole Proprietorships are not eligible.
How does Brex make money?
Brex doesn’t charge you fees or interest. Instead, we simply receive a small fee from the merchant for each purchase you make with your Brex card.
What are the payment terms?
Each day of spend has a 60-day float. Brex will charge you for today’s spend in 60 days, and tomorrow’s spend in 61 days. 100% of the amount due on the 60th day must be repaid.
How is my credit limit determined?
Brex determines your credit limit up to 75% of your projected monthly sales, forecasted using the sales data you provide through your Shopify integration (or similar sales platform). Companies can get credit limits up to $5 million.
Will applying for Brex affect my personal credit score?
No. Applying for Brex, whether your application is approved or rejected, will never affect your personal credit score.
Are there any additional fees?
Nope. Unlike with traditional loans, merchant cash advances, working capital loans, or most other financing options, Brex doesn’t charge fees or interest, period.
