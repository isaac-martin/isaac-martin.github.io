How do I redeem points?
You can redeem points for business travel via Brex Travel, or you can redeem points through the dashboard for a statement credit or cash back on any transaction.
Do points expire?
No. Brex points never expire, and theres no minimum or maximum redemption value required.
How many points can I earn?
Theres no cap on what you can earnas your company grows, Brex grows with you. Unlike other card programs that cut off your multipliers after you hit a certain spending limit, Brex rewards multipliers are always uncapped.
How do I join Brex Exclusive?
Brex Exclusive is a Rewards status that gives you access to the highest multipliers available, automatically applied to your account when you make Brex the exclusive corporate card for your business. Eligibility is determined on a rolling, monthly basis.

All companies who enroll in Brex for Life Sciences automatically have Brex Exclusive Rewards for their first two months.

The Brex Exclusive rewards program includes enhanced rewards, point multipliers, and other benefits that may not be provided to all Brex users. A company with a Brex account in good standing that uses and commits to continue to use Brex corporate cards for all of its company-related card purchases may be permitted to participate in the Brex Exclusive rewards program. Companies that make purchases on behalf of third parties (such as purchasing a ride share for your customers) may not qualify for Brex Exclusive. Brex determines eligibility by using the information provided to Brex from the company and third-party providers.
